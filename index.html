Order Terms

Pick Up & Order Policy

Please read carefully before placing your order. By submitting an order, you acknowledge and accept the policies below.

🍥
Why we're strict: Every roll is baked fresh to your order using real ingredients and a lot of love. A missed pick up means product, time, and ingredients are lost and cannot be recovered. These policies help us respect our work and keep prices fair for everyone.
01

Pick Up Days & Time Slots

+

All orders are baked fresh for your specific pick up slot.

You select your pick up day and time when placing your order. Your rolls are prepared and held specifically for that slot.

Pick up slots:

  • Friday: 10:00am – 1:00pm
  • Friday: 4:00pm – 6:00pm
  • Saturday: 9:00am – 12:00pm

The exact pick up address in Pretoria will be confirmed via WhatsApp once your order is accepted.

02

15-Minute Grace Period

+

We offer a 15-minute grace period after your selected pick up window closes.

If you arrive within those 15 minutes, your order will be waiting for you as scheduled.

03

If You're Running Late

+

Life happens — we get it! If you know you'll be late, you must contact us on WhatsApp (082 563 0046) before your pick up time or within the 15-minute grace period.

If you reach out in time and our schedule allows, we will do our best to:

  • Hold your order for a later same day pick up, or
  • Work out an alternative solution where possible.

Without communication, your order may be discarded once it is outside the safe holding window.

04

Missed Pick Up (No Show / No Call)

+

A missed pick up is when you do not arrive within your selected time + 15-minute grace period and you do not contact us within that time frame.

In this case:

  • Your order has already been baked and prepared just for you.
  • Because our products are perishable and made to order, they cannot be re-sold.
  • Once the holding window has passed, your order is discarded for food safety and quality reasons.
05

Refunds & Replacements

+

No refunds, re-bakes, or transfers to another date are available for missed pick ups (no show / no call).

The work, ingredients, and time have already gone into your order, and it has been prepared specifically for you.

If your rolls do not meet our quality standard when you collect them, please let us know immediately so we can make it right.

06

Weather & Other Circumstances

+

We understand that Joburg/Pretoria weather and traffic can be unpredictable. However, these do not automatically qualify for a refund or free replacement.

As long as you contact us before or during your pick up window, we will do our best to assist with a solution.

If we don't hear from you until after your window and grace period have passed, the missed pick up policy above applies.

07

Last Minute Orders

+

Pick up days have limited capacity. When a slot is full, we cannot accept more orders.

Orders placed within 24 hours of your selected pick up day are considered last minute orders. We often begin production immediately, which means:

  • We reserve the right not to issue a refund for last minute orders under any circumstances including change of mind, scheduling conflicts, or forgetting to collect.
  • By placing a last minute order, you acknowledge that you are taking one of our limited production spots and agree that the sale is final.
08

Courtesy Goodwill Gesture

+

On rare occasions and at our discretion only, we may offer a goodwill gesture (e.g., a discount on a future order) as a token of appreciation for loyal customers.

This is never guaranteed and does not replace the no refund policy for missed pick ups. We will always try to go the extra mile but only when it's within our means to do so.

Questions? WhatsApp us on 082 563 0046 before placing your order — we're always happy to help. 🍥

Our Story

A Family Affair, Baked in Pretoria

Sinnamon Rolls SA started the way all the best things do — with too much butter, a very messy kitchen, and a batch of cinnamon rolls that were dangerously good.

We're a family-run, small-batch home bakery in Pretoria. No mass production, no shortcuts, no frozen dough. Every single roll is hand-rolled, generously filled, and baked fresh to your order by us.

What started as a weekend kitchen experiment quickly turned into something our family, friends, and neighbours couldn't stop asking for. So here we are.

"Sinfully Good" isn't just our tagline. It's our promise to you.
